What is fertilization?

 

QUESTIONS

1.What is fertilization?

2.The male cells of flowering plants are called  -----------.

3.The female sex cells in flowering plants are called------------.
4.A fertilized ovule forms a ------------.
5.A mature ovary forms a  ------------.

 

ANSWERS

1.Fertilization is  the fusion of the male sex cells and the female sex cells.
2.Pollen
3.Ovule
4.A seed
5.A fruit
