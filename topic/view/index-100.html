Weather changes

We often observe, talk about and want to know about the weather every day in our daily activities.

Weather is how the day looks like.
It can be sunny, cloudy, rainy, windy or calm
Weather can change all the time.
On a sunny day, the sun is very hot.
People drink a lot of water and animals stay under the shade.
When it is rainy, people carry umbrellas.
It becomes muddy.
When it is windy tress are blown from side to side.
Strong wind can blow off roofs of houses and uproot trees or break branches.
On a cloudy day, there are many clouds in the sky.
There is no sunshine.
Big dark clouds bring rain.
When it is calm, there is no wind.
The trees do not sway from side to side and smoke rises up straight.

What is weather?

Weather is how sunny, rainy, cloudy or windy any moment of a day can be.

However, if there is no wind blowing the day is referred to as a calm day.

We observe weather using all our senses.

Weather changes

The condition of the day can change every second.

  • A sunny day is a day that the sun is shining

 

  • A rainy day is a day that we have rain

  • A cloudy day is a day having clouds

  • A day is windy if there is wind blowing

 

  • A day is calm if there is no wind blowing at all.
