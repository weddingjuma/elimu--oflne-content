Vocabularies: Travell  by  Olpha Janduko

What is the meaning of the following words?

Mechanic

What are they doing?

Puncture

What has happened here?

Petrol station

What is this place for?

Ticket

Bus station

Which  place is this?

Conductor

Who is this?

Board

What are they doing ?

Luggage

What can you see on top the vehicle?

Passengers

What are they doing?

Engine

What is its function?

6

Answers

Mechanic a person who repairs machines like vehicle engine.

Puncture a small hole in a tyre causes pressure to escape.

Petrol station is cars are to be filled with petrol.

Ticket a printed piece of paper that gives you a right to travel on a particular vehicle.

Bus station a place where buses stop.

Conductor a person who collects money from passengers or check their tickets.

Board to get on a bus.

Luggage a bag or case containing things of the passenger when travelling

Passenger a person who travels in a bus.

Engine is the part of a vehicle that produces power to make it move.
