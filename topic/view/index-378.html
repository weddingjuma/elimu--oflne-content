Compound interest

Compound interest can be calculated using the simple interest formula.

To do this the amount for the previous year becomes the principal for the succeeding year.

The Formula

Example

Masaba borrowed Sh 120 000 from a commercial bank. He paid the loan in 2 years.

The bank charges compound interest at the rate 18% per annum.

Calculate the amount paid.

 

Solution

Using the formula I = P x (R/100) x T

 

1st year interest =120 000 x  (18/100) x 1

 

Amount = 120 000 + 21 600 = 141 600

 

2nd year interest = 141 600 x (18/100) x 1 = Sh 25 488

 

Total interest = Sh 21 600 + Sh 25 488 = Sh 47 088

 

Amount = 1st year principal + total interest

 

= Sh 120 000 + Sh 47 088

= Sh 167 088
