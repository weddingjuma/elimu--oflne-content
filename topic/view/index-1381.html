How many seconds are in the following minutes?

  • 1minute

  • 5minutes

  • 10minutes

  • 60 minutes

 

Solution

(a) 1minute
1minute=60 seconds
Therefore 1minute =  1 ×   60
                                   = 60seconds

 

( b) 5minutes
1minute= 60 seconds
5minutes= 5x60
                  300 seconds

 

(C) 10minutes
1minutes =60 seconds
10 minutes = 10 x 60
                   = 600 seconds

 

( d ) 60 minutes
1minute= 60 seconds
60minutes= 60x 60
                     =3600 seconds
