Nouns ending with 'y' preceded by a vowel.

•We add 's' when forming plurals.
•Add 's' to these nouns.
 
1.Boy
2.Key
3.Monkey
4.Toy
5.Valley

 

Answers.

1.Boys

2.Keys

3.Monkeys

4.Toys

5.Valleys

Nouns ending with 'y' preceded by a consonant.

•We remove 'y' and add 'ies' when forming plurals.
•What is the plurals of the following nouns.

4.Puppy
5.Lorry

2.Berries

Using apostrophe

Apostrophe( ' ) can be used in two ways.

 

1.To show ownership.

The book of the teacher.

• The teacher's book.

The car of my father.

•My father's car.

The key of the clerk.

•  The clerk's key.

The sufuria of the cook.

•The cook's sufuria.

The lorry of the lady.

• The lady’slorry.

 

Exercise

Where do we put the apostrophe in sentences below?

  • The jembe of the farmer.
  • The pen of the pupil.
  • The house of the doctor.
  • The dress of the girl.
  • The phone of the teacher.

 

Answers

  • The farmer's jembe.
  • The pupil's pen.
  • The doctor's house.
  • The girl's dress.
  • The teacher's phone.

 

To show a letter is missing in a word.

  • It is ____ It's
  • Did not ____ Didn't
  • Have not ____ Haven't
  • I would ____ I'd
  • Will not ____ Won't
