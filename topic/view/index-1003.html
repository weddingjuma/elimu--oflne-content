Checking for Understanding (CFU)

This video will help teachers understand a teaching strategy called "Checking for Understanding" it is a powerful strategy that will guide instruction and increase student engagement. CFU will improve instruction in turn improving student achievement CFU is the teacher continually verifying that students are learning what is being taught while it is being taught. CFU allows you to make instructional decisions during the lesson, monitor student progress in real time so as not to wait until formal assessments in order to revise lessons. To ensure that students are not practicing and reinforcing mistakes. Practice makes permanent, not perfect. CFU ensures student engagement by continuous checking if the student understands and is on task. This video will help teachers learn how to implement this powerful teaching strategy that will provide ongoing informal assessment in real time.
