Vocabularies: Celebration  by  Grace Githongori

6

What is the occasion?

A celebration is when people from a community get together to remember and share a special event.

What is a congregation?

A congregation of worshippers.

Who is a bride?
A bride is a woman who is about to be married or who is newlywed to a man.
Who is a bridegroom?
Who are groomsmen?
Groomsmen are the male attendants to the groom in a wedding ceremony.

6

Who are bridesmaids?

The bridesmaids are members of the bride's party who are often  close friends or sisters. They attend to the bride on the day of a wedding.
Bouquet of flowers
A flower bouquet is a collection of flowers in a creative arrangement
Reception
Reception is a standup for social event with food and beverages, arranged to welcome one or more special guests or a newly married couple.

A wedding reception

What is a wedding gown?
  • Wedding gown is the clothing worn by a bride during a wedding ceremony.
Who is a master or mistress of ceremony?
  • A master of ceremonies (also known as an MC or emcee) is the official host for a staged event, performance, or party. Normally, a master of ceremonies introduces speakers, makes announcements, and engages with the audience to keep the ceremony agenda flowing as smoothly as possible.
