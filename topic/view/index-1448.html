What is a language group ?

This is a group of people who speak almost the same language.

 

Look at the following map of Eastern Africa

Do you know the language groups that mainly occupy this area?

The language groups that occupy Eastern Africa are:

  • Bantus
  • Nilotes
  • Cushites
  • Semites

Bantus original homeland was the congo forest

They migrated from Congo forest in search of fertile land

NILOTES

 

6

Nilotes are divided into three groups, which are;

  1. Highland nilotes
  2. Plain nilotes
  3. River lake nilotes
They came from South Sudan in bahr-el-Ghazal region.

The main economic activities of the nilotes include:

 

Highland nilotes are farmers
 
Plain nilotes were pastoralists
 
River lake nilotes practised fishing
 

THE CUSHITES

6

Their main economic activity is pastoralism

That is why they move from place to place in search of water and pasture.

Cushites are divided into;

  • Eastern cushites
  • Southern cushites

 

THE SEMITES

They are a mixture of Arabs and Jews.

They originated from Asia
