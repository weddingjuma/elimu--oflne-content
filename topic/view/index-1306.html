QUESTIONS

What is the meaning of place value?

What is the meaning of total value?

 

ANSWERS

Place value is the position where a digit is in a number.

Total value is the product of a digit and its place value.

 

TOTAL VALUE EXAMPLE

What is the total value of digit 4 in the number? 7834509

 

SOLUTION

The place value of digit 4 is thousands Its total value is: 4 *1000=4000

Therefore the total value of 4 is 4000
