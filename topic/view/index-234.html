What is Traditional African Heritage?

Traditional:

It means a practice or belief that is passed down from one generation to the next

  • African:

This refers to what has its origin or source in the continent of Africa.

  • Heritage:

This means the inheriting of customs, beliefs, practices or ways of doing things from a previous generation and continuing in the same ways.

  • Traditional African Heritage is therefore, the beliefs, moral values and practices that distinguish us from one another.
  • In the traditional African society the future of a society depended on its young people.The youth were educated and brought up to take up their duties and roles in the society.
