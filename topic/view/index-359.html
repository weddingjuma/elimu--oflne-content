Constructing angles of 90º and 45º

Constructing angles of 60º, 30º and 15º

Constructing 60º

Procedure

(i) Draw a line AB.

(ii) Draw an arc with centre A and of any radius to cut AB at N.

(iii) Draw another arc with centre N and radius AN to meet the other arc at M.

(iv) Draw AM, angle MAN = 60º.

To construct angle of 30º bisect 60º. To construct angle of 15º bisect 30º.
