G.C.D is also referred to the Highest Common Factor (H.C.F) G -stands for greatest C- stands for common D- stands for divisor GCD refers to the largest common factor that can be divisible into a number without a remainder, or the highest common factor that can divide a number without a remainder. What is a factor? These are numbers we can multiply to get another number. For example 4 x 5 = 20. 4 and 5 are factors of 20 while 20 is the product. Therefore 4 and 5 are divisors of 20.

What are the divisors of 20?
Divisors of 20 are 1, 2, 4, 5, 10 and 20

 

  • Note; that  1 x 20 = 20

  • 2 x 10 = 20

  • 4 x 5 = 20

  • Therefore 1, 2, 4, 5, 10 and 20 are factors of 20

  • Divisors are numbers that divide another number evenly without a remainder

 

Example 1

what is the GCD of 12,24 and 36

( i) list down the factors of each number.
( ii) list common factors in both numbers.
(iii) Identify the Highest/greatest common factor/divisor

 

Solution

12; 1,2,3,4,6,12

24; 1,2,3,4,6,8,12,24
36; 1,2,3,4,6,9,12,18,36


The common factors are; 1,2,3,4,6,12
The greatest common divisor is 12
