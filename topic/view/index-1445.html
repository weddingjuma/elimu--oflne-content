Impact of climate change on human activities  by  Joab Otieno

6

What is climate ?

Climate is an average weather condition of a place recorded over along period of time 30 to 35 years.

 

6

What are the impacts of climatic change on human activities?

  • Shortage of pasture results to migration of pastoralists

  • Water shortage in rivers affect electricity generation.

  • Prolonged drought may lead to death of animals

  • Perennial flooding discourages tourism.

 

 

6

Apart from the what you have seen flooding can also cause?

Exercise

State four impact of climatic change on human activities.

Answers

  1. Shortage of pasture.
  2. Water shortage on rivers.
  3. Perennial flooding discourages tourism.
  4. Displacement of people.
  5. Electricity generation is affected due to shortage of rainfall.
