  • tens of thousands
  • thousands

Therefore the number 7,982,495 is read as seven million, nine hundred and eighty-two thousand, four hundred and ninety-five. 
write the following numbers in figures and symbols or figures
Two hundred and thirty-four thousand, six hundred and fifty-seven.
Two million, seven hundred and eighty-nine thousand, four hundred and three.
Seventeen million, eight hundred and thirty-four thousand, and nine.

 

The commas indicate the category of numbers.Example the number768,502

  • starting from right 2 is ones place, 0 is tens place,5 is hundreds place, the set represents ones.
  • The number 8 is thousands place,6 is the ten thousand place and 7 is the hundred thousand place, the set represents thousands.

  • How much is a hundred million?

  • ten groups of ones(1)=10

  • ten groups of tens(10)=100

  • ten groups of hundreds(100)=1,000

  • ten groups of thousands(1000)=10,000

  • ten groups of ten thousands(10,000)=100,000
