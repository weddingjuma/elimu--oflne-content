What is the reciprocal of the following numbers.

2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10?

  • Reciprocal is what you multiply a value by to get 1.

  • Its called multiplicative inverted.

  • This simply means, turning number up- side -down.

  • Remember whole numbers are always the numerator while 1is the denominator.

  • Therefore 2/1=1/2, 3/1=1/3,4/1=1/4,5/1 =1/5

  • 6/1 =1/6, 7/1=1/7 ,8/1 =1/8, 9/1= 1/9 and , 10/1 =1/10
