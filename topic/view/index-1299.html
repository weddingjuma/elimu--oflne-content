Writing numbers  by  Mary Komen

6

The place value guides us to read numbers and symbols.

 

EXAMPLE:

7,982,495

 

  • 7 :millions
  • 9:hundred of thousands
  • 8:tens of thousands
  • 2:thousands
  • 4:hundreds
  • 9:tens
  • 5:ones

 

Therefore the number 7,982,495 is read as seven million, nine hundred and eighty-two thousand, four hundred and ninety-five.

  • How much is a hundred million?
  • ten groups of ones(1)=10
  • ten groups of tens(10)=100
  • ten groups of hundreds(100)=1,000
  • ten groups of thousands(1000)=10,000
  • ten groups of ten thousands(10,000)=100,000
  • ten groups of  hundred thousands(100,000)=1,000,000
  • ten groups of  millions(1,000,000)=10,000,000
  • ten groups of ten millions(10,000,000)=100,000,000
