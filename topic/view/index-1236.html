    Once upon a time,there lived two children in a village called Bora.These children were neighbours and good friends.They schooled in the same school.

    The two friends used to steal other chidren’s pens, books and pencils.The teaches tried to advice them but the message fell in deaf ears. On reaching class seven they fell out of school.

    In the village they started stealing hens, goats and cows. One day they met a woman who was from town with luggage they followed her and robbed her everything.

    The woman ran to the nearby police station and reported the incidence. She gave the directions the gangs took. Without wasting time, the policemen started searching for them following the directions they were given.

    In a shake of the lamb’s tail they were found resting under a tree since the luggage was heavy. The police officers were helped by their dogs.

    The police officers apprehended them and took them to the police station where they recorded a statement. They were locked up in a cell to wait to be judged. After two days, they were taken to the courtroom where they were judged on robbery with violent because there was evidence.The two were sentenced death.
