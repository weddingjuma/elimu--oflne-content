Planning

Plan in Good Time

"If you fail to plan, then you're planing to fail!"

First think about your timetable

  • How much time have you got?
  • What do you need to do?
  • Be realistic about the targets you set yourself and how much time you will need.

Select:

  • What topics do you need to revise?
  • How much time do they need?

 

Balance between subjects you’re strong in and those which you’re less confident about.

A week in hand, aim to have your revision completed by a week before your exams. This gives you:

  •  flexibility in case of illness
  •  a chance to spend longer on something that proves difficult
  •  a break before you sit your exams.
